EXPLORE THE DIFFERENT ROLES THAT ARE WAITING FOR YOU

Imperial Intelligence Agent

As an Imperial Intelligence Agent, you will be responsible for gathering critical intelligence, monitoring potential threats to the Empire, and conducting covert operations. You'll work closely with other agents and intelligence officers to ensure the security and stability of the Galactic Empire.

Inquisitor

The Inquisitor is a highly trained and Force-sensitive operative employed by the Sith Order or the Galactic Empire. Tasked with tracking down and eliminating Jedi survivors, the Inquisitor plays a crucial role in maintaining Sith dominance and suppressing any remnants of the Jedi Order.

Imperial Army Scout

Scouts are skilled in reconnaissance and gathering intelligence. They operate behind enemy lines, providing valuable information about enemy movements and positions to aid in strategic decision-making.
